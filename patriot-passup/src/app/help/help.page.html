help Posting an Item: Enter the name of the item you are selling, the price, and the description. The description itself automatically contains the quantity like "I wish to sell a pack of 7 pencils" There is a load picture button where you can select a picture of your item from your folder. After you are done, click on the confirmation button. Buying an Item: There is already a long page where you can scroll up or down on the current items for sale There is a search bar function where you can take in the necessary keywords to filter through the item collection If you type in "Calculus" it would display all items containing that name. After you found your desired item, click on the item to send a request to the seller. As the seller you will see a buyers gmu email appear in your chats tab with the item they are interested in buying. It will be up to your discretion to contact the interested student via email. That is how to complete a transaction. Happy passing up! Back
